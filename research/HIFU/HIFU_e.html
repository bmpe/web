Development of High Intensity Focused Ultrasound Robotic System

Introduction

High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU)
    High intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU) is a promising way of medical treatment for cancers due to its minimal invasiveness and safty. However, skin burn, long treatment time and incomplete ablation are main shortcomings of this method. MR guided system is effective for checking the effect of ablation but the cost of system is very high and the monitoring is not realtime. In this research, we develop HIFU robotic system with US guid which can moving insonification, using this system we can achieve effective ablation. Goal of this study is to develop minimally invasive epicardial electrophysiological mapping system that can be integrated with surgical robot system in order to realize more precise diagnosis, therapy and evaluation.
