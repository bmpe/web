Development of the bone cutting device
using an ultrasonic scalpel

The present condition and the research purpose

　Cutting of a bone at the hip joint and the leg is generally widely performed in the field of plastic surgery now. But the surgeon cuts a bone with the gouge and the hammer, and skillful technique is required for an operation. For simplification of an operation and minimum invasive, we try to mechanize maintenance and drive mechanism. In this research, we aims at making unnecessary the skillful technique and realizing minimum invasive in the bone cutting operation.

The method of this research

　In this research, we used an ultrasonic scalpel as what was replaced with a gouge or a hammer in order to realize minimum invasiveness in the bone cutting operation. The ultrasonic scalpel is shown in Fig 1. The main problem of it is generating of heat in cutting born. Therefore we have developed the mechanism which could cut born minimum invasively by controlling cutting conditions, such as power and speed. The device is shown in Fig. 2.

Fig. 1: Ultrasonic scalpel Fig 2: Drive mechanism

The present result and a future view

　With the first prototype, the heat did not generate over 40° by controlling the cutting load below to 200 [gf]. Fig. 3 shows a cutting plane of bone using a ultrasonic scalpel. The prototype could cut the bone in the shape of a plane. We will improve the device to cut the bone in shape of a surface of a sphere.
